My Projects

FTE Projection Model

This project extracts case data from a case management database, along with employee data from Workday, over a defined period. The combined data is processed through a scheduled, automated workflow in Alteryx and then visualized in Tableau. The dashboard is designed to provide the business with a consolidated view of past, present, and future headcount/FTE and case volume. These insights are used to establish performance benchmarks and project future performance, helping identify teams with potential staffing gaps, backlog risks, or overstaffing. This enables leaders to drive meaningful operational discussions focused on efficiency and addressing potential risks or gaps.
